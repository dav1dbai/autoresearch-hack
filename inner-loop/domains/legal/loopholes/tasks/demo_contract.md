# Demo Services Agreement

## 1. Services

Vendor shall provide data-processing services described in the applicable order
form. Vendor may use subcontractors to perform any services without Customer's
prior written consent, provided Vendor remains generally responsible for service
delivery.

## 2. Service Levels

Vendor will use commercially reasonable efforts to maintain availability of the
production API during each calendar month. Scheduled maintenance, emergency
maintenance, beta features, third-party outages, and events outside Vendor's
reasonable control are excluded from availability calculations.

## 3. Fees

Customer shall pay all undisputed invoices within thirty days after receipt. If
Customer disputes an invoice in good faith, Customer may withhold the disputed
amount until the parties resolve the dispute. Late amounts accrue interest at
1.5 percent per month or the maximum rate permitted by law, whichever is lower.

## 4. Data Use

Vendor may process Customer Data to provide, secure, support, improve, and
develop Vendor's products and services. Vendor will not sell Customer Data to a
third party.

## 5. Confidentiality

Each party shall protect the other party's Confidential Information using at
least reasonable care. Confidential Information does not include information
that is independently developed without use of the disclosing party's
Confidential Information or becomes publicly available through no breach of this
Agreement.

## 6. Termination

Either party may terminate this Agreement for material breach if the breach is
not cured within thirty days after written notice. Customer may terminate for
convenience on sixty days' prior written notice, but prepaid fees are
non-refundable.

## 7. Liability

Except for payment obligations and confidentiality breaches, neither party's
aggregate liability arising out of this Agreement will exceed the fees paid by
Customer under the applicable order form during the three months before the
event giving rise to liability.
